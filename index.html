Alireza Falah

I have an MSc in Robotics and Automation from Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) in Budapest. My research focuses on Environment Perception (Cameras, LiDARs, Radars) in Robotics. Previously, I worked as a Research Engineer at HUMDA Lab Non Profit Ltd, where I contributed to Reinforcement Learning for Trajectory Optimization and later to developing simulation scenarios for autonomous vehicle racing using C++, Python, and ROS2.
